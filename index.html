Our Objective

The Monke Token advocates for a return to a simpler and better time. A time when people lived in harmony with one another and worked for common good. We plan to achieve this by building a community that will work together for a set of common goals that we believe will help humanity and the planet as a whole by putting us closer with our Ape Bretheren. These goals are defined below:

Our Goals

Max Pixel

An Open Exchange

The open exchange of ideas and goods, all through MONKE. We hope that our community will exchange goods with and reward each other by using the Monke Token as an asset. This will primarily be done through our Discord Server, which will be an open place for discussion and collaboration.

International Fund for Animal Welfare Animal Rescue

Environmental Awareness

The Monke community will work endlessly to ensure that our world is safe from the environmental hazards plaguing it. Monke is built on the Algorand Blockchain, which is carbon-negative and energy efficient thanks to its innovative Pure-Proof-of-Stake Protocol.

Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden

Justice for Harambe

The Monke Community will help ensure that zoo animals, especially apes, are well taken care of. We want to ensure that nothing like Harembe's death ever happens again. "Justice for Harembe" can be achieved by teaching the community that great apes are gentle creatures that we should cherish, and strive to be like.

Tokenomics and Roadmap

The Tokenomics for MONKE are quite simple: 70% was locked in the TinyMan DEX for exchange, while 30% is held in the "bannana reserve" to facilitate development, rewards, and community engagement.
